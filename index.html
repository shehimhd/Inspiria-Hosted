    FAQ

    • What is Inspiria'22?

      Inspiria is the most awaited national flagship event conducted by IEDC MESCE annually. Inspiria focuses on developing a sense of confidence among students to pursue their dreams and boost their entrepreneurial spirit. This includes a set of events containing inspirational talk sessions and technical workshops by public personalities, experts and influencers.

    • Who can attend Inspiria'22?

      Absolutely open to all! No matter what course you're in, Inspiria'22 would be worth attending!

    • Is IEDC membership mandatory?

      No. Any student can register and be a part of Inspiria'22.

    • Do you have roam around provisions?

      Definitely. We have included roam around option to accommodate the interests of those who wish to visit stalls.

    • Do you provide an accommodation facility?

      Yes. We'll be arranging accommodation facilities for boys and girls in our respective hostels.
